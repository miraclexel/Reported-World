Reported World
This is a quick mock up of key parts of the website with a working title: Reported World. The intention of this mockup is to enable the collection of feedback from stakeholders about the concept of Reported World. Reported World aims to create a comprehensive overview of every world problem, who's working to solve the problem, and what they're doing, all on one platform. It will do this by taking every video report about world issues and connect it to all the organisations solving the video report's problem. It aims to be an invaluable resource for journalists to find and share information, NGOs to connect and find new supporters, and the public to stay informed and support NGOs. Please ensure you're on a computer for this guide to work properly.
